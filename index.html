About

I am a research associate at the Institute for Information Science and Natural Language Processing (IwiSt), University of Hildesheim, Hildesheim, Germany.

For my PhD in Computational Linguistics, I am currently researching the integration of explicit knowledge into neural NLP methods for short text classification (such as hate speech detection in social media).
