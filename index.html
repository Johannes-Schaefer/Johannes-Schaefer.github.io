About

I am a research associate at the Chair for Fundamentals of Natural Language Processing and the Bamberg NLP-Group (BamNLP) at the University of Bamberg. My research in the CEAT project focuses on the analysis of event chains. I am investigating the automatic recognition of an event sequence and the cumulative emotion sequence using prompt tuning/optimization methods.

Previously, I was a research associate at the Institute for Information Science and Natural Language Processing (IwiSt), University of Hildesheim, Hildesheim, Germany. For my PhD in Computational Linguistics, I am researching the integration of explicit knowledge into neural NLP methods for short text classification (such as hate speech detection in social media).
